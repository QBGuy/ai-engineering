## Mermaid diagrams

Whenever creating, editing, restyling, or reviewing a Mermaid diagram in this repository, load and follow `.codex/skills/mermaid-styling/SKILL.md`.

## Writing style

Be clear and concise. Use the fewest words that fully convey the point. Cut filler, redundant explanations, and restatements. Prefer short sentences and direct language.
